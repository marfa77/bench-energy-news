China's Dec met coal imports rise 28% MOM to 13.8 mil mt

AI Summary: China imported 13.77 million metric tons of metallurgical coal in December, up 28.3% from November. Mongolia was the largest supplier to China, with 6.72 million mt in December, up 7.6% from November, accounting for 48.8% of China's total imports for the month. Australia's volumes more than doubled month over month, rising 109.3% to 1.78 million mt, while Canada's volumes were up 110% to 1.10 million mt.

China's metallurgical coal imports surged 28.3% in December to 13.77 million metric tons, driven by strong Mongolian supplies and significant increases from Australia and Canada, signaling robust industrial restocking and potential market stabilization.

Supply Breakdown by Country
