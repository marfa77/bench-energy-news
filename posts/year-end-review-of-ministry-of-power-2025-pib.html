India's Power Ministry: Comprehensive Coal Supply Review for 2025

Domestic Coal Performance Highlights

The Ministry of Power has released a comprehensive review demonstrating exceptional coal supply management during fiscal year 2025, with several critical achievements:

  • Successfully met peak electricity demand of 242.49 GW in June 2025
  • Significantly improved domestic coal availability
  • Discontinued mandatory imported coal blending advisory

Strategic Implications

The ministry's performance indicates a robust shift towards energy self-sufficiency and reduced import dependency.

"Our domestic coal strategy has proven both resilient and adaptive," - Ministry of Power Official
