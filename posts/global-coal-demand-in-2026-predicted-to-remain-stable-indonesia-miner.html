Global Coal Demand in 2026: Stability Signals Market Maturity

Market Overview

The Indonesian Coal Mining Association (APBI) has projected a remarkably stable outlook for global coal demand in 2026, with China and India expected to demonstrate modest growth between 0.2% and 1%.

Key Market Metrics

  • Demand Growth: 0.2% - 1%
  • Indonesia's Market Position: 55% of China's coal imports (November 2025)
  • Price Trends: ICE Newcastle coal at $107.5/ton

Bench Energy Expert View

What this means: The coal market is demonstrating remarkable resilience, with Asian economies maintaining strategic energy portfolios despite global transition pressures.

Market impact: Expect incremental price movements and stable supply chains across key Asian markets.

  • Price implications: Minimal volatility expected
  • Supply chain effects: Consistent Indonesian export volumes
  • Regional dynamics: China and India maintaining steady imports

Risks & Opportunities: Potential for gradual market adjustments, with Indonesia positioned as a key strategic supplier.
